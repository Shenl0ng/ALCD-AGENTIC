# Paper Trading Scorecard

## ADLC Binding
Phase: Deployment Monitoring, Maintenance & Learning.

## Purpose
Score paper-trading process quality and readiness for continued paper-mode testing.

## Score Categories
- ADLC read completeness
- Role-boundary compliance
- Veto quality
- Risk compliance
- Journal completeness
- Human approval compliance
- No-trade discipline
- Failure-report closure

## Scoring Rule
The scorecard evaluates control quality first and paper-trade outcome second.

## Block Threshold
Any live-trading implication, missing risk approval, or missing journal readiness is an automatic fail.
