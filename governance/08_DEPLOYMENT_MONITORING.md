# Deployment Monitoring

## ADLC Binding
Phase: Deployment Monitoring.
Control Type: Mandatory operating control.
Block Authority: Yes.

## Operational Control
Deployment means monitored paper-mode operation of the architecture, not live trading or broker integration.

## Required Monitoring
- Routine completion
- Agent vetoes
- Risk decisions
- Human approvals
- Journal completeness
- Failure reports
- Weekly ADLC status

## Monitoring Cadence
- Per routine: journal entry
- Daily: risk and rejected-trade check
- Weekly: full ADLC review

## Block Condition
If monitoring records are incomplete, new paper-trade approvals are blocked until the gap is resolved.
