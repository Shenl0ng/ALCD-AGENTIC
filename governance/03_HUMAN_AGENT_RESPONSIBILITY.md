# Human-Agent Responsibility

## ADLC Binding
Phase: Human-Agent Responsibility.
Control Type: Mandatory operating control.
Block Authority: Yes.

## Operational Control
Humans remain accountable for strategy adoption, risk limits, approval rules, and any future decision to implement software.

## Human Responsibilities
- Define and approve risk limits
- Approve changes through change control
- Decide whether paper-trade recommendations are acted on outside this architecture
- Review weekly ADLC findings

## Agent Responsibilities
- Follow assigned role only
- Produce auditable outputs
- Veto unsafe or incomplete workflows
- Preserve paper-only boundaries

## Block Condition
If accountability cannot be assigned to either a human owner or an agent role, the action is blocked.
