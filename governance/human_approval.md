# Human Approval

## ADLC Binding
Phase: Human-Agent Responsibility.
Control Type: Mandatory operating control.
Block Authority: Yes.

## Operational Control
Human approval remains mandatory wherever governance, risk, or paper-trade authorization rules require it.

## Approval Required For
- Risk limit changes
- Strategy rule changes
- Autonomy expansion
- Any paper-trade authorization requiring discretionary confirmation
- Any future move toward implementation

## Approval Record
Approval records must include date, approver, scope, decision, and linked proposal or change request.

## Block Condition
Missing approval means `NO_TRADE` or `EXECUTION_BLOCKED`, depending on workflow stage.

Human approval cannot substitute for risk approval, journal readiness, paper-mode confirmation, or specialist agent sequencing.
