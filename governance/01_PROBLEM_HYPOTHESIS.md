# Problem Hypothesis

## ADLC Binding
Phase: Preparation & Hypothesis.
Control Type: Mandatory operating control.
Block Authority: Yes.

## Operational Control
The system exists to test whether a multi-agent ADLC process can improve paper-trade selectivity by rejecting weak setups before risk or execution gates.

## Hypothesis
A controlled multi-agent workflow with specialist vetoes will produce fewer, higher-quality paper-trade approvals than a monolithic trading agent.

## Required Evidence
- Rejected-trade records
- Approved paper-trade records
- Journal completeness
- Risk compliance
- Weekly ADLC review

## Block Condition
If the system optimizes for trade frequency or paper PnL without process quality, the hypothesis is no longer being tested.
